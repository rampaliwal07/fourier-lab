🎯 Aim of the Experiment

1. Understand the concept of Fourier series representation of periodic signals.

2. Learn to compute Fourier coefficients for standard waveforms (square, sawtooth, triangle, half‑rectified sine).

3. Observe how the half‑period L affects the harmonic frequencies.

4. Recognize the difference between sine and cosine series for even/odd functions.

5. Apply Fourier series in engineering contexts like signal processing and vibrations.


By the end, you will be able to write the Fourier expansion of any periodic function given its coefficients.
