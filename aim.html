Aim of the Experiment

Understanding Fourier Series

1. To understand the representation of periodic functions as an infinite sum of sines and cosines.

2. To compute Fourier coefficients for standard waveforms (square, sawtooth, triangle, half‑rectified sine).

3. To observe how the half‑period L affects the harmonic frequencies.

4. To analyze even/odd symmetry and its effect on the series (only cosine or only sine terms).

5. To apply Fourier series in engineering problems like signal analysis and vibrations.
